02 — PROBLEM STATEMENT

Challenges in Traditional Food Aid Distribution

Bangladesh distributes food aid to millions of vulnerable citizens — but without technology, the system is riddled with inefficiencies.

👻

Ghost Beneficiaries

Deceased, duplicate, or fabricated beneficiary registrations receive rations without any physical presence or biometric verification.

~8.4%
estimated ghost entries
🏪

Dealer Fraud

Unscrupulous dealers under-distribute food, sell on the black market, collude with fake beneficiaries, and falsify distribution records.

~4.1%
verified leakage rate
📝

Manual Record-Keeping

Paper-based registers are easily manipulated, lost, or destroyed. No real-time visibility into distributions, stock levels, or beneficiary status.

72 hrs
average reporting lag

05 — MODULE SHOWCASE

Nine Integrated Modules

Each module is purpose-built for a specific function, fully integrated through shared services and real-time data.

📊

National Dashboard

Real-time KPIs, live distribution feed, and program overview for national-level monitoring and quick action.

🏢

Offices & Programs

Manages the full administrative hierarchy from National HQ to 4,892 distribution centers across 495 upazilas.

🏪

Dealer Management

License registration, compliance scoring, suspension workflows, and field visit scheduling for all 1,247 dealers.

👥

Beneficiary Registry

Enroll beneficiaries with biometric capture, assign quotas by category (Widow/Elderly/Poor/Disabled), and manage eligibility.

📦

Food Distribution

Biometric identity verification at point-of-distribution with real-time transaction recording and offline sync capability.

🔍

Field Inspection

6-point compliance checklist, inspector assignment, automated scoring, and instant report submission from the field.

📈

Analytics & Intelligence

ML-driven demand forecasting, anomaly detection, leakage alerts, and program-wise performance breakdowns.

🔔

SMS Notifications

Bulk Bengali-language messaging to beneficiaries and dealers. Pre-built templates, scheduling, and 95%+ delivery rate.

🔒

Audit Trail

Blockchain-style immutable event log with cryptographic hash chaining. Every action recorded, tamper-proof and exportable.
